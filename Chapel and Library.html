Chapel and Libary

Every school days morning, students go to the chapel where annoucements are given by teachers and students. It's one of the building that, when occupied, is for most of the time filled with the whole school. Next to it, we have our library. It's filled with books of all kind. Most study periods are held in there. For boarding students who need to work on group projects, it's the best place to work in.
