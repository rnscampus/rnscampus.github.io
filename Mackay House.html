Mackay House

This building is the senior boys dorm. It contains 24rooms distributed on 3 floors. There are 3 single rooms and all the rest of the rooms are doubles. The basement of the building has a common room with a TV and couches, and a pool table. There is also a kitchen in the basement and a room with a Ping=Pong table. There are common bathrooms on each of the top 3 floors.
